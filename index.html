Moving from discretionary trading to systematic execution requires a shift in mindset. We replace "I feel" with "The data says."

What is Algo Trading?

Algorithmic trading is the process of using a pre-defined set of rules (an algorithm) to execute trades. Instead of clicking buttons based on intuition, the system monitors market conditions and executes according to strict logic.

Emotionless Execution: Never tired, scared, or greedy.
Backtesting Power: Validate on years of data in seconds.

What are Quants?

"Quants" (Quantitative Analysts) are the architects of modern finance. They use mathematical models and statistical analysis to identify profitable patterns in price action and volume.

"A Quant doesn't guess where the market is going; they calculate the probability of where it could go."
