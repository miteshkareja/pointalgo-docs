What is TradingView?

TradingView is the gold standard for cloud-based charting. It allows you to analyze financial markets, apply indicators, and track price action in real-time — all from your browser or mobile device without heavy software installation.

Key Advantages

  • Advanced Multi-Timeframe Charting
  • Thousands of Open-Source Strategies
  • Real-time Global Market Data
  • Custom Pine Script (v5) Support
  • Instant Cloud Alerts & Webhooks
  • Seamless Web & Mobile Sync

What is MetaTrader 5 (MT5)?

MetaTrader 5 is a high-performance professional trading platform built for stability and execution. It is the platform of choice for running automated systems (Expert Advisors) across Forex, Indices, and Commodities.

Platform Strengths
