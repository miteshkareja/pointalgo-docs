Gann-Pi Matrix

A proprietary price-time matrix combining Gann Square of 9 Geometry with Pi Cycle Time Theory to pinpoint institutional reversal levels.

Unlike standard support and resistance, the Gann-Pi Matrix dynamically calculates future pivot zones based on mathematical degree increments (45°, 90°, 180°). It then overlays a 314-period time cycle to alert you exactly when price and time are in perfect alignment for a high-probability reversal.

Algorithm Features

  • Price Geometry: Dynamic Support/Resistance mapped via Gann Degrees.
  • Pi Cycle Convergence: Mathematical time zones based on 3.14 (314 bars).
  • Real-Time Alerts: Signal triggers when Price and Time align on key levels.

Quant Benefits

  • Noise Filtration: Filters out erratic price movement by focusing on structural pivots.
  • High-Fidelity Entries: Increases win-rates by requiring time-price confluence.
  • Systematic Rule-Book: Eliminates emotional trading with a strict IF/THEN execution model.

Market Compatibility & Use Case

The Gann-Pi Matrix is universally applicable. It calculates geometry based on the asset's own volatility, meaning the logic scales perfectly across any market environment.
