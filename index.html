Price Geometry

The indicator calculates dynamic Support/Resistance levels using W.D. Gann’s mathematical increments. It utilizes a **Degree Step** (e.g., 45°, 90°) to project price levels from historical pivots.

Time Cycles

Using the **Pi ($\pi$) Cycle Period (314)**, the indicator plots a sine wave over the price action. Reversals are most likely to occur when price hits a Gann level exactly when the Pi wave hits its peak threshold.

Signal Logic (Cross Edition)

LONG

Price Crossover S1 Support

Wait for price to cross ABOVE the primary S1 level while the Pi cycle is in the "Green Time Zone".

SHORT

Price Crossunder R1 Resistance

Triggered when price falls BELOW the primary R1 level while the Pi cycle is in the "Red Time Zone".

Parameter Guide

Setting Default Impact
Pivot Lookback 20 Determines the anchor high/low for level calculations.
Degree Increment 45.0 Sets the sensitivity of the Gann grid. 45° is standard for intraday.
Pi Cycle Period 314 The historical cycle based on 3.14. Lower numbers = faster signals.
